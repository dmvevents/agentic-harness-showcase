The road to GA.

From v0.2.4 today to v1.0.0 / GA — an honest, evidence-gated plan. GA isn't declared; it's earned by a reproduced live cluster bring-up, a knowledge loop that's actually closed, and real benchmark data — with every claim sourced and every spend human-gated.

What "not yet GA" honestly means. The live multi-node bring-up (--mode prod) has never run end-to-end (capacity-gated); the benchmark repo holds 0 real measured runs yet; and ~1,900 mined, categorized learnings sit in the review lane awaiting human-gated promotion. The capacity assistant (G-KR4) shipped. GA closes the rest — with proof, not claims.

The bar

What GA means

cluster_ops is GA when an unfamiliar operator can, from one goal sentence, autonomously and safely bring up + validate a real multi-node HyperPod EKS GPU cluster, get a provenance-backed report, and tear it down — with every claim sourced and every spend/mutation human-gated — reproducibly, twice.

Deferred to post-GA (by design): the restricted-portal auth tier (SSO/RBAC), full inference-catalog depth, and Salesforce/opportunity integration.

Immediate next step

Close the loop before mining more

The highest-value next action needs no capacity: promote a curated batch of the highest-signal mined learnings to canonical (v0.3.0 / G-KR2). Extraction produces latent value; promotion realizes it. We stop net-new mining until a batch is promoted — and because promotion to canonical is human-gated by design, that's the one step that needs a person in the loop.
